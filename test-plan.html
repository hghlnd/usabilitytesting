Test Plan

The purpose of this test plan is to evaluate the usability of the Khan Academy platform, focusing on navigation, accessibility, and user engagement.

Objectives

  • Assess the ease of navigation and task completion for both learners and teachers.
  • Identify potential usability barriers and areas for improvement.
  • Evaluate user engagement features such as feedback and progress tracking.
  • Test accessibility settings and their visibility.
  • Compare usability across desktop and mobile platforms.

Tasks

  1. Sign-Up Process: Evaluate how intuitive the sign-up process is, including password creation.
  2. Homepage Navigation: Test the clarity of the homepage and ease of locating subjects, lessons, and features.
  3. Starting a Lesson: Assess the process of selecting a subject, grade level, and starting a lesson.
  4. Feedback and Progress Tracking: Test the feedback mechanisms during quizzes and the visibility of progress tracking tools.
  5. Switching Between Subjects: Evaluate how easily users can switch subjects during lessons.
  6. Accessibility Settings: Determine how easily users can locate and utilize accessibility settings.
  7. Search Functionality: Test the effectiveness and efficiency of the search bar for finding specific content.
  8. Account Recovery: Evaluate the ease of resetting a password and account recovery process.
  9. Mobile vs. Desktop Comparison: Compare usability between desktop and mobile platforms, including navigation and lesson completion.
  10. Quiz Evaluation: Assess the structure and difficulty of quizzes and user preferences for feedback timing.

Post-Test Questions

Participants will be asked the following questions after completing all tasks:

  • What did you like most about the platform?
  • What was the most challenging aspect of using the platform?
  • Are there any features you think could be improved or added?
  • Would you recommend this platform to others? Why or why not?

Metrics

  • Time taken to complete tasks.
  • Number of errors encountered.
  • User satisfaction with the process.
  • Observations of user behavior and verbal feedback.
